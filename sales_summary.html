Methodology & Data Notes

Source: live HubSpot CRM query for owner Jack Holmgren, June 1 – July 17, 2026 (run July 17, 2026), plus Jack's LinkedIn "connected" export for the same window.

Email cleaning: 1,332 raw EMAIL engagement records were logged this window. 71 were calendar/meeting-sync noise (invitations, accepted/declined notices, recurring syncs) and 494 were duplicate log entries created when Gong re-logs a touch already sent through the email tool (same subject, same day). Both were excluded, leaving 767 real prospecting sends. Sequences map to HubSpot sequence IDs 306852915/306852912 (CMX Survey / Quick Hello), 306459277 (Quick Note / CMX Report), and 306973038/306972434/306972431/306972487 (Personalized 1:1); no-sequence sends are Ad-hoc/Untracked.

Calls: pulled directly from the CALL object, joined to the associated contact's company.

Accounts touched: CONTACT records created by Jack Holmgren in the window, joined to associated COMPANY for platform/tier/target. A few contacts are linked to more than one company, and some call contacts (e.g. at Hughes) were created before this window, so counts here won't sum exactly against the 516 new-contact total above.

LinkedIn: 263 total connections in Jack's "Connected on" export for this window, shown as a headline count only.
